About

RealTalk is a browser extension made to fight against AI slop. As video generation tools become more and more popular and smarter, it is increasingly hard to tell what is AI content and what is not. RealTalk uses advanced technology to scan a video's transcript for AI. Stop wasting time on content generated by a robot.

RealTalk is made by David, a high school innovator from Hunter College High School in New York City.
